How to use Ice Cream

🗺️

Interactive Map

The map shows broken ice cream machine rates across every U.S. state, coloured by severity.

  • Click a state to zoom in and see individual McDonald's locations as dots.
  • Orange dots are broken machines. Teal dots are working.
  • Click anywhere on the map background to zoom back out to the U.S. view.
  • Use the Broken Rate % / Total Locations toggle above the map to switch the colour scale.
  • Hover over any state to see a summary tooltip with exact counts.
📊

Stats Strip & City Table

Below the map you'll find live summary stats and a ranked table of the 20 cities with the most broken machines right now. Data refreshes automatically from mcbroken.com.
